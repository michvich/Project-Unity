Project Unity

Project Unity is a movement to bring people together, no matter their background, beliefs, or location. We strive to create an understanding and mutual respect amongst all members of society.

Mission statement

Our mission is to bridge the gaps between cultures, age groups, and geographical boundaries. We strive to create an environment where people of all backgrounds feel comfortable and respected.

Programs

Project Unity offers a variety of programs to foster inclusion and understanding. We offer educational programs, cultural exchange programs, and community outreach programs.

Volunteer

Project Unity is always looking for volunteers to help promote our mission and programs. We need volunteers to help us spread the word, plan our events, and even assist in developing new programs. If you are interested in becoming a volunteer for Project Unity, please fill out the form below.



Events

Project Unity hosts a variety of events throughout the year to bring people together and promote our mission. We organize discussion groups, community outreach activities, and cultural activities. Check back often to see upcoming events.

Donations
